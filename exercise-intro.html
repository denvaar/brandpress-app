Branding

Branding is about making an emotional connection with your audience. It's a feeling that transcends your product or service whenever they interact with you. The BrandPress will help you begin the search for that emotion and meaning. It will lead you to your true identity.

Getting started

The BrandPress consists of four sections that contain exercises to help you determine the attitude and attributes you aspire to portray in your new brand. Answer each question truthfully and with your ideal brand in mind.

All the questions correlate to heavily researched psychology theories including archetypes, personalities and human motivation. Once you complete all the questions, submit your answers and we'll send you a report of your initial brand archetype and personality. This report can help guide marketing materials and establish your brand voice. It's also extremely helpful for creating an acurate and meaningful brand logo.

Please contact support@brandpress.io if you have questions or would like assistance completing the exercises.
