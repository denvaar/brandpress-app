Automobile Analogy

Pretend your new brand is something else entirely.

In this exercise

For the Automobile Analogy, you will pretend your new brand is something else entirely. Automobiles inherently possess the ability to portray the attitude or personality of the driver. That's why it seems odd to see a rather tall individual in a compact car. People identify with the autombobile they drive. Can you identify the car your new brand would drive if they were a real person?
