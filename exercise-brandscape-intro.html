Brandscape Exercise

Understanding who you admire and who you compete with.

In this exercise

The brandscape in which your new brand will reside is viewed in context to national brands and competitors. National brands are well known and many consumers will have previous experience with them. Are there any national brands you admire or have positive characteristics you could possibly emulate in your own brand? Competitors are the brands your target market are comparing you to directly. What are the strengths and weaknessess of their brands? What can you do to differentiate yourself from them?
