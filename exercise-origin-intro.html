Origin Story

Let's start with some business details before we get to the fun stuff.

In this exercise

The origin is the backstory of your brand. How did you get started? What are the key moments in the history of this new brand? Understanding who you are and where you came from is one way to build a strong narrative. A narrative that is honest and relatable will help create brand loyality.
