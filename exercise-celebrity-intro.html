Celebrity Analogy

Pretend your new brand is something else entirely.

In this exercise

Celebrity endorsements are a great way to literally put a face to a brand. Celebrities are generally associated with a termperment or personality they have depicted to the general public. This image is consciously transfered to the brand they endorse. If you could have anybody represent your new brand, who would it be?
